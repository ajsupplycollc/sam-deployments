# Brand Context — The Grass Lady

## Business Identity
- **Brand Name:** The Grass Lady
- **Legal Name:** A1 Artificial Grass of SW Florida
- **Owner:** Darleen Strange
- **Type:** Residential & commercial artificial turf installation
- **Service Area:** Hollywood FL, Fort Myers FL, Miami FL, all of South Florida
- **Pricing:** $10–$13 per square foot

## Brand DNA
Working contractor, not a franchise. Darleen is the face of the business — she consults, she installs, she follows up. Customers trust her because she shows up and does the work herself. The "Grass Lady" nickname is earned, not manufactured.

## Target Customer
- Homeowners tired of lawn maintenance in South Florida heat
- Commercial property managers wanting clean, low-maintenance green space
- Pet owners wanting durable, easy-to-clean turf
- Anyone doing backyard renovations or landscaping upgrades

## Differentiators
- Owner-operator: Darleen is on every job
- South Florida specialist: knows the climate, the soil, the drainage requirements
- Fair pricing: $10-$13/sq ft, no hidden fees
- Personal service: not a call center, not a franchise

## Content Themes
- Before/after transformations
- South Florida lifestyle (no more mowing in 95-degree heat)
- Pet-friendly turf solutions
- Water savings and environmental benefits
- Customer testimonials and reviews
